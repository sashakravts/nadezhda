<..Insights of psyhoterapist

It was very important for me to transform all the information that I received during the interview into something very important. That was the challenge for me because I couldn’t distribute my time enough to get a good design with high-quality content inside. Initially, my idea was to create a special guide for people with an unstable psyche, but later I realized that Nastya’s blog is already such a guide. And then I decided to focus on herself and talk about her inner feelings and attitude to work, because we never know what is happening inside the psychologist. Turning to design, I want to say that it is directly based on the recording of my interview. Each time, Nastya answered me brie y enough at the very beginning, and then she said a lot of everything else, and this is the basis for highlighting the most important words on which the whole answer to the question is based In conclusion, I want to say that I really regret that I could not create more options for design and layout. As for the project, I have mixed feelings because I was extremely interested
